No any internal problem in the party – Doctor Sharma

He expressed his gratitude to all the seniors who had contributed to the party and worked for a long time, saying that there had been an internal competition at the party's convention.

  NewsKhabar      November 4, 2022







KATHMANDU: Dr. Sunil Sharma, Nepali Congress candidate in Morang Constituency No. 3, has stated that the party has no internal problems. In an interview with reporters today, he stated that one person’s dissatisfaction is normal in a party with 1.2 million active members. He stated, “I cannot say that I do not intend to join the Democratic Party after the party nominates an official candidate.” He expressed his gratitude to all the seniors who had contributed to the party and worked for a long time, saying that there had been an internal competition at the party’s convention. That is also a lovely feature of democracy. In the practice of democracy, there is competition. Some people win, while others lose. We experienced the same thing. He will not, however, get all of the opportunities if he loses, and he will not get all of the opportunities if he wins. Dr. Sharma recalled that on the day he arrived at the election site, he held everyone’s ear and begged forgiveness, saying, “The door of dialogue with friends is not closed.”
